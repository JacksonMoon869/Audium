How It Works

Audium enables incarcerated individuals to make free phone calls by listening to short, ethical audio ads before connecting. Here's the simple 3-step process:

  • 1. Inmate dials out using the Audium platform.
  • 2. A 15–30 second audio ad is played — brand-safe, targeted, respectful.
  • 3. The call connects — no charge to the inmate or their family.

Why It Matters

Audium restores communication access, dignity, and support to those most isolated. Studies show that regular family contact reduces recidivism, promotes rehabilitation, and lowers emotional stress. We ensure no one is cut off because they can't afford a call.
