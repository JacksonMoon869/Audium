How It Works

Audium enables incarcerated individuals to make free phone calls by listening to short, ethical audio ads before connecting. Here's how:

  • 1. Inmate dials out.
  • 2. A 15–30 second ad plays.
  • 3. The call connects—completely free.

Why It Matters

Every call keeps families connected, reduces recidivism, and eases financial burdens. Audium brings dignity and communication to those who need it most.
